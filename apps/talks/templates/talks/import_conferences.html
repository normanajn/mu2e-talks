Import Conferences from CSV

Expected CSV format

The CSV must have these columns (order does not matter):

inspire_id,title,start_date,end_date,url

Dates must be YYYY-MM-DD or blank. Existing conferences with a matching inspire_id are updated; new ones are created.

Generate a compatible CSV with the fetch-inspire-conferences script:

./scripts/fetch-inspire-conferences --start-date upcoming --pages 4 -o conferences.csv
